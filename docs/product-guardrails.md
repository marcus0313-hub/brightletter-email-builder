# En Route Product Guardrails

This prototype follows the provided Product Bible brief as the active source of truth because no separate Product Bible file was present in the workspace.

En Route is scaffolding, not dependency. It should deepen the relationship, not become the relationship.

The emotional center is: "We notice each other more deeply."

Language should feel warm, grounding, emotionally spacious, and lightly structured. Prefer words like rhythm, moment, intention, reconnect, grounding, momentum, return, tending, partnership, repair, season, reflection, connection, seen, and felt.

Avoid product choices that feel like grading, surveillance, partner comparison, dense analytics, social feeds, or a replacement for human conversation.
